For timeline labs, we built a world-class sentiment analysis tool that helped news organizations and celebrities understand the sentiment and level of user engagement throughout the world. During the course of this project, we:

  • Increased file upload capacity and reliability while meanwhile significantly decreasing file upload time allowed for greater customer satisfaction and retention
